Whether you're here to enjoy the bard's enchanting melodies, partake in a friendly game of cards, or share your own tales of adventure, there's always something happening at the Tipsy Troll Tavern. Join us to experience the warmth, flavors, and stories that make the Tipsy Troll more than just a tavern—it's a destination. Your seat by the fire is waiting.

About us

Welcome to the Tipsy Troll Tavern, where the warmth of a roaring hearth meets the laughter of adventurers and locals alike. Nestled in the heart of the village, our tavern is a timeless retreat, offering a refuge from the trials of the outside world. Whether you're an adventurer returning from a long journey, a local looking for a cozy spot to unwind, or a traveler seeking a taste of the village's finest brews, the Tipsy Troll is the place to be. Step inside, and you’ll be greeted by the comforting glow of wooden beams and the crackling of a stone hearth, where stories are shared, laughter echoes, and friendships are forged. Our tavern’s walls are adorned with relics from distant lands—trophies, weapons, and trinkets from travelers who’ve left a piece of their journey behind. It's these stories that make the Tipsy Troll a place of magic and wonder. At the heart of the Tipsy Troll lies our dedication to serving exceptional brews. From our legendary Dreamweaver’s Delight to the rich, amber notes of Troll’s Gold Ale, we pride ourselves on crafting drinks that leave a lasting impression. But we don’t stop at great drinks. Our kitchen is filled with the aromas of hearty meals, from rich venison stews to freshly baked honey-glazed bread—perfectly paired with your favorite brew. As the sun sets, the tavern comes alive with music, storytelling, and games. Join us by the fire for an evening of merriment, or share your own tales of adventure with fellow travelers. Whether you're enjoying the tunes of a wandering bard, engaging in a friendly game of cards, or simply enjoying the company of good friends, there’s always something happening at the Tipsy Troll Tavern. Come for the brews, stay for the stories, and leave with memories that will last a lifetime. Your seat by the fire is waiting.
